Horimiya characters




About the show: It is an anime show that was aired on 2021. It is all about the relationship of Hori and Miyamura and their friends. It is a romance/slice of life type of Anime and its cool.













Hori










Hori, full name Hori Kyouko is one of the main of the series and her boyfriend is Miyamura.











Miyamura










Miyamura, full name Izumi Miyamura, is also one of the main in the series and his girlfirend is Hori











Yuki










Yuki, full name is Yuki Yoshikawa, is one of the sub-main characters in the show and she is one of many friends in the show and that she has a crush on Toru.











Toru










Toru, full name is Toru Ishikawa, is also one of the sub-main characters in the show and he has a crush on Hori but rejected him.
